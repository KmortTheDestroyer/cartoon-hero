About Deidara

Deidara is a member of the criminal organization Akatsuki, known for his ability to create explosive clay sculptures. He takes great pride in his art and is a skilled fighter who uses his creations to devastating effect in battle.

Powers & Abilities

  1. Deidara can create and manipulate Explosive Clay.
  2. He has complete control over detonating his clay and can use it to create powerful attacks, such as his C1, C2, and C3 techniques.
  3. Deidara sees his Explosive Clay creations as a form of art and takes great pride in their design and execution.
